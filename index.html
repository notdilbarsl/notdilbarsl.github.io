Grid Runner

1. Enter your User ID.
2. Select the Size of the Grid and the Number of Mines.
3. Click "Start Game" to begin and navigate the grid.
4. If you step on a mine, you lose, so avoid the mines.
5. Start from the top-left corner.
6. Reach the bottom-right corner to win!
